About E-lafda

This is a two player game where you have to fight, collect coins, and battle for victory in 150 seconds. WASD and arrow keys are used for movement for the 2 players. K and X are attack players/chests. V and P to use powerup (if u have it) on your opponent. Q and L to throw chappals at your enemy (there are two types of them) collect cchests for later they might be helpful.

Bogas Space Adventure

this is a single player game where you have to help Boga fight off enemies and find his way across space. A and D are used for 2d movement and spce bar is used to jump left click is used for light attack and right click is used for heavy attack X is used to summon projectiles C to dash and V for arc wave.

Tic-Tac-Toe-Toe

This is a simple command-line Tic Tac Toe game implemented in Python. Two players take turns to mark X or O on a 3x3 grid. The first player to align three of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins. The game ends in a draw if all squares are filled without a winner.
